About Blind, Deaf, and Mute Individuals

Blind, deaf, and mute individuals face unique challenges in their daily lives. Technology has made significant strides in improving their quality of life.

Explore the interactive sections below to learn more about assistive technologies.

Screen Reader System

Screen readers are software applications that convert on-screen text into synthesized speech. Users can navigate web pages, read books, and access digital content. Learn More

Sign Language Interpreter

Sign language interpreter apps and devices help deaf individuals communicate with others who may not know sign language. Learn More

Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC)

AAC devices assist mute individuals in expressing themselves using symbols, pictures, or text-to-speech. Learn More
